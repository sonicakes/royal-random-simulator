# Royal Simulator — Gemini Image Prompts
## Style Reference
Eastern European art house cinema poster illustration, Polish Film Poster School 1960s–70s style, referencing the work of Franciszek Starowieyski and the visual language of Valerie and Her Week of Wonders (1970). Surrealist painterly composition, bold unexpected colour combinations, slightly distorted dreamlike figures rendered with painterly detail against flat graphic backgrounds. Atmospheric, unsettling, and theatrical. Muted jewel tones — deep burgundy, forest green, ochre, slate blue — with one vivid accent colour per image. A small glowing green diamond-shaped crystal floats above the protagonist's head. Square or portrait composition. Full bleed image — no border, no frame, no white margin, no vignette. Colour and figures extend to every edge. The image must have no surrounding white space whatsoever. Treat the entire canvas as the painting. No text.
